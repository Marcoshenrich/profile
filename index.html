Background and Resume

My Story

I'm a multinational raised in the United States, Brazil, and Ecuador. I moved to the United States permanently to attend Bard College at Simon's Rock starting at just 16 years old.

After working for years as a Production Manager at a printing facility, I moved to California to join a startup that needed someone with a sense of process and direction to bring structure to the chaos.

In 2017 I joined the team at Sunbasket and stayed with them through five years of growth and challenges. During this time, I supported the Creative and Marketing teams to develop our business processes, asset production pipelines, brand bibles, and to roll out new product lines. I was promoted three times as our teams and product lines grew, and I was brought in to the PMO to help drive meaningful organizational change. 

In 2022 I got my PMP and decided to pursue software engineering to increase my ability to drive impact and join teams and projects that contribute real human value. I was accepted to my bootcamp of choice after just a month of learning to code. I learned to build well-designed web applications and saw how much there is still to learn about software engineering.

I am seeking to join a mission-driven company that delivers quality products that help people. I am so excited to bring my newfound technical skills coupled with my breadth of experience to my next role.
